## Governed Uses (Conditions Apply)

The following uses are permitted **only if provenance and intent are preserved**:

- Commercial reuse of documentation or templates
- Inclusion in datasets used for training models
- Large-scale aggregation or redistribution
- Derivative frameworks or services built from these materials

In these contexts, users are expected to:
- retain attribution
- preserve references to this repository
- respect stated intent and scope
- acknowledge downstream value creation

Attribution should include, where reasonable:
